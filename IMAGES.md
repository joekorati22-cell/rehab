## 🎨 Image Guidelines

### Format Requirements
- **Photos**: JPG (compressed, 80-90% quality)
- **Transparency**: PNG (for images with transparent backgrounds)
- **Logos**: SVG (scalable vector graphics)

### Quality Standards
- **Resolution**: High-res, web-optimized
- **File Size**: 100-300KB per image (compressed)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Maintain recommended dimensions
- **Naming**: Lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `knee-care.jpg`)

### Optimization Tips
1. Compress images using TinyPNG, ImageOptim, or Squoosh
2. Convert to WebP format for better compression (optional)
3. Use lazy loading for below-the-fold images
4. Ensure professional quality and consistency
